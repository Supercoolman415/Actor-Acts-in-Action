Welcome to the World of "Actors in Action"

Acting as career involves more than just performing in movie. Working actors perform in live theater producitons, for examples commericals and on television shows. As actors start theird careers, many work multiple jobs, such as working as extras in films or TV, to support themselves financially. Travel is often necessary, and competition for acting position is extremely keen.


The more experience an actor has, the more desirable he or she is to a director. A degree is not required, bacelor's degree programs in drama and theater arts are available.
